Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your privacy and data while using the IRPCS Navigator application.

Information Collection

Local Data Processing

The IRPCS Navigator application processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Quiz Data

When you generate quizzes, your quiz preferences and results remain on your device. No quiz data is stored on external servers.

Cookies & Storage

Local Storage Usage

We use browser local storage only to save your preferences, such as:

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Quiz configuration settings
  • UI customization options

This data remains on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Downloadable Content

Generated Documents

When you generate quiz documents:

  • All document generation happens on our server
  • Documents are temporarily stored only for the purpose of facilitating download
  • All temporary files are automatically deleted immediately after download
  • No personal information is embedded in the generated documents

Your Rights & Choices

Control Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Clear browser data to remove all stored preferences
  • Use incognito/private browsing to prevent storage of any data
  • Delete downloaded files from your device when no longer needed

Contact Information

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
